Photoshop Bridge Project

In preparing for this project, we would go outside in groups and take photos of bridges, both literally, symbolically and metaphorically.

The assignement was to take one or more photos and edit according to requirements.

The requirements included the following:
Remove element from photo, add element to photo, adjust brightness, crop photo to fit ratio 4:3 and adjust size of the photo to be no more than 1 M pixels.

The photo on the on the left is the finished result.
It has been edited to include a submarine in the Copenhagen canal.
